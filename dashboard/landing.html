THE PROBLEM

Your team already uses AI. Your data is already leaving.

An employee pastes a customer's QID, IBAN, and balance into a public chatbot to draft a reply. That data may now sit on a server outside the country.

Cloud redaction is too late.

By the time a cloud tool can sanitise data, it has already crossed the border.

Global tools are English-first.

They miss Qatari QID, Gulf IBAN and SWIFT formats, and Arabic names.

"Trust us" is not residency.

A contractual promise on a foreign cloud does not satisfy a regulated audit.

THE SOLUTION

A control, not a chatbot.

Wemik sits before the model. It minimises sensitive data at the source, then gives risk and compliance a verifiable trail.

On-device redaction

Raw sensitive data never crosses the trust boundary.

Arabic and GCC-native

Detects QID, Gulf IBAN, SWIFT, and Arabic names that global engines miss.

Fail-closed with human review

If Wemik is not sure, it asks. It does not silently miss.

Audit-ready by design

Every redaction decision can be logged for risk and compliance.

HOW IT WORKS

Sensitive data stays on the device. The AI still does the work.

  1. 01

    On device.

    An employee types or pastes a message containing customer data.

  2. 02

    Local redaction.

    Wemik detects and tokenises it on-device: QID becomes [ID_1].

  3. 03

    Only the redacted prompt is sent.

    The model receives a sanitised prompt, never the raw values.

  4. 04

    Restored locally.

    The response comes back and real values are re-inserted on the device.

The model works on a sanitised prompt. Raw entities never leave. That is a control your auditors can verify, not a promise.
